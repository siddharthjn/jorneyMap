  • Overcoming Limitations

    One of my achievement in my project was to work on a very non-standard request from PNS to convert hourly employees on open work schedules and making them salaried pay from hourly pay. It was not a recommended best practice as we advised PNS, however, they persisted in requesting this change. This solution was very important for business because they were going to hire/update over 300 Seasonal employees. The complexity of the solution increased because these employees did not have fixed bi-weekly hours. This was a long standing ticket which through my tenacity I was able to close; this was quite imperative for the team and client as well. I did require a lot of direction since it required very advanced and deep expertise, exploring pay period salary proration and factoring. We noted down all the assumptions taken in consideration for the configuration and created documents for business to make them aware of the new solution going to take place. Following the IBM Practice “Put the client first” I took up the challenge, did an enormous amount of testing and outlined for the client what SAP can do and what limitations exist.
