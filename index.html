  • Bluemix Hackathon

    Partnered with Universities in Halifax to organize an Internet Of Things Bluemix Hackathon. Through this Hackathon we were able to promote and train students in bluemix, align fellow IBMers with CAMSS strategy & recruit young talent from the universities
    I was awarded Service Excellence Award and given the opportuinity to lead the Bluemix Hackathon Initiative across IBM Centers in North America.

  • Blue TIPSS

    As a team of new graduates, we built a framework that would engage, enhance & retain new hires.
    The framework based on IBM's grow organic strategy won the Best Vision Award at the GIC We Deliver Competition and as a team we were nominated for Service Excellence Award

  • Microsoft Hack A Week Winner

    Using Microsoft's technoligies like Azure, Netduino & Twilio API, our team built a 2 step verification system called Authnet. Through this 35$ solution, you could let your friends access your apartment through AuthNet's webapp, receive notification if someone tries to break in and see the logs of who accessed your apartment

    • Project

  • Workstation Setup/Documentation

    Before joining Panorama, it used to take 1 month for a new developer on the team to setup his workstation. As a team of new graduates in Halifax, we documented every problem,steps and solutions with screenshots on the wiki. This drastically reduced time from 1 month to mere 3-4 days to setup the workstation.
    As a result we were able to cut down the cost by 3 weeks/resource enabling them to start delivering after being for 1 week on the account.

  • Overdelivering for 2.5.7 Beta Release

    As a team of early professionals in Halifax, we were faced with a daunting task of delivering critical solutions for the 2.5.7 release.
    We leveraged the senior resources based in Burnaby and coordinated with our counterparts in India only to found out that we had overdelivered vs the client's expectations. As a team, we recieved special mention from scrum master and clients for this accomplishment.
